SPACECOOL INC. is a Japanese climate-tech company pioneering passive radiative cooling materials that work without electricity. Our SPACECOOL® material reflects sunlight and emits heat to the outer space, enabling surfaces to cool below ambient temperature. Applied to buildings, warehouses, tents, and portable cabins, it reduces indoor heat by up to 10°C and lowers cooling energy use by 30-40%, contributing to cost savings and CO₂ reduction.

A flagship project was carried out at the Emirates Palace Mandarin Oriental Hotel in Abu Dhabi, where a large tennis court tent was covered with SPACECOOL membrane. Even under peak summer heat, the facility maintained comfortable conditions without air cooling —showcasing sustainable outdoor leisure in the Gulf region.

With proven results in Japan, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and Thailand, SPACECOOL is scaling globally. Our mission is to deliver zero-energy cooling solutions that support both climate change adaptation and mitigation.
